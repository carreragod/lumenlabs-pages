FAQ

How do I import text?
You can paste text directly, import TXT/PDF/EPUB files via the file picker, use the clipboard button, or share content from other apps (Safari, Notes, WeChat, etc.) using the iOS Share menu.
Does VoiceReader need an internet connection?
No. All text-to-speech processing happens on your device. VoiceReader works completely offline.
What languages are supported?
VoiceReader supports Chinese and English, including mixed Chinese-English text.
Can I listen with the screen locked?
Yes. VoiceReader supports background audio playback. You can also control playback from the lock screen and Control Center.
How do I adjust the reading speed?
Tap the speed button (e.g. "1x") in the reader view to choose from 0.5x to 2.0x. You can also set a default speed in Settings.
What is the sleep timer?
The sleep timer automatically stops playback after a set time (15, 30, or 60 minutes). Great for listening before bed.
Does VoiceReader collect my data?
No. VoiceReader collects zero data. Your text never leaves your device. See our Privacy Policy for details.
The app is large. Why?
VoiceReader bundles a neural network TTS model (~80-100MB) on-device so it can work offline with high-quality voices. This is what makes it sound natural without needing the internet.

System Requirements

iOS 17.0 or later. iPhone with arm64 processor.
