Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Overview

VoiceReader is designed with privacy as a core principle. All text-to-speech processing happens entirely on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.

Data Collection

VoiceReader does not collect any data. Specifically:

  • No text you import or read is ever sent to any server
  • No usage analytics or tracking
  • No personal information collected
  • No cookies or advertising identifiers
  • No third-party analytics SDKs

On-Device Processing

All text-to-speech synthesis is performed locally on your device using a bundled neural network model. No internet connection is required for any functionality.

File Access

VoiceReader accesses files only when you explicitly import them (via file picker or Share Extension). Imported text is stored locally in the app's private container using SwiftData.

Third-Party Services

VoiceReader does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that collect user data.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If we ever update this policy, changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
