ScreenZen Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-03-17

Overview

ScreenZen is designed to be offline-first. We do not collect, store, or sell personal data.

Data Collection

ScreenZen does not collect:

  • Names, emails, phone numbers
  • Precise location
  • Device identifiers for tracking
  • Analytics / advertising identifiers

Data Storage

Your data is stored on your device. We do not operate any servers to store your content.

Third-Party Services

ScreenZen does not integrate third-party analytics or advertising SDKs.
