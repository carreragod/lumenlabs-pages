Frequently Asked Questions

How does receipt scanning work?

Point your camera at a receipt and SnapLedger will use OCR and AI to extract the amount, merchant, date, and category automatically. You can review and edit the details before saving.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, it syncs across your Apple devices via your personal iCloud account. We never have access to your data.

Do I need to link my bank account?

No. SnapLedger does not require or support bank account linking. All transactions are added manually or via receipt scanning.

What's included in SnapLedger Pro?

  • AI receipt scanning and auto-categorization
  • Full category breakdown charts
  • Spending trend analysis
  • Home screen widgets
  • CSV export
  • Free 1-month trial included

How do I restore my subscription?

Open SnapLedger → Settings → Restore Purchases. Your subscription is tied to your Apple ID and will be restored automatically.

How do I export my data?

Go to Settings → Export Data. Your transactions will be exported as a CSV file that you can open in Excel, Numbers, or Google Sheets.
