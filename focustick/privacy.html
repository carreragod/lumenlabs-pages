FocusTick 隐私政策

生效日期：2026 年 2 月 28 日

概述

FocusTick 尊重并保护您的隐私。本应用不收集、存储或传输任何个人数据到外部服务器。

数据收集

FocusTick 不收集任何个人信息，包括但不限于：

  • 姓名、邮箱、电话号码
  • 位置信息
  • 设备标识符
  • 使用行为分析数据

数据存储

您的专注记录、标签、成就等数据仅存储在：

  • 您的设备本地存储
  • 您的个人 iCloud 账户（如已启用 iCloud 同步）

我们无法访问您的任何数据。

第三方服务

FocusTick 不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK。

音频素材

应用内的白噪音音频来源于 Pixabay，使用 Pixabay License（免费商用，无需署名）。音频文件内置于应用中，不涉及网络请求。

儿童隐私

FocusTick 不针对 13 岁以下儿童，也不会有意收集儿童的个人信息。

政策变更

如本隐私政策有更新，我们会在此页面发布新版本。

联系我们

FocusTick Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 28, 2026

Overview

FocusTick respects and protects your privacy. This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.

Data Collection

FocusTick does not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email, phone number
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics

Data Storage

Your focus sessions, tags, achievements, and other data are stored only on:

  • Your device's local storage
  • Your personal iCloud account (if iCloud sync is enabled)

We have no access to any of your data.

Third-Party Services

FocusTick does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs.

Audio Assets

White noise audio files are sourced from Pixabay under the Pixabay License (free for commercial use, no attribution required). Audio files are bundled within the app and do not require network requests.

Children's Privacy

FocusTick is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Policy Changes

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be published on this page.
